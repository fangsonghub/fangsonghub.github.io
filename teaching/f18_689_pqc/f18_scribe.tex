Instructions on github (\url{https://github.com/CQuIC/qcircuit}). A
simple example here:
\[
\Qcircuit @C=1em @R=1em {
& \ctrl{2} & \qw & \gate{H} & \ctrl{1} &
\gate{H} & \qw \\
& \qw & \qw & \gate{H} & \targ &
\gate{H} & \qw \\
& \targ & \qw & \gate{Z} & \qw & \ctrl{-1} &
\qw
}
\]
